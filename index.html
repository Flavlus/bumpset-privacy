Health & Safety Disclaimer

BumpSet is a workout-tracking tool only. It does not provide medical advice, physiotherapy, or professional training programmes, and using it is entirely at your own risk.

Weightlifting carries a risk of injury. The sets, reps, and weights shown in the app are suggestions and beginner starting points only. They do not account for your individual fitness level, health, or technique. Only lift what you can handle safely with correct form, and never more than feels controlled.

If you feel sharp pain, dizziness, or anything that feels wrong, stop immediately. If you are new to training, returning from injury, or have any health condition, consult a qualified fitness professional or doctor before starting.

To the fullest extent permitted by law, the developers of BumpSet accept no liability for any injury, illness, or harm resulting from use of the app. By using BumpSet you accept these risks and take full responsibility for your own safety.

Privacy Policy

BumpSet ("the app") is a workout tracker. This section explains what data the app handles. In short: everything stays on your device, and we collect nothing.

Data we collect

None. BumpSet has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or advertising SDKs. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal or usage data.

Data the app stores on your device

Your workout templates, logged sets, and preferences are saved in a local database on your device only. This data never leaves your phone unless you choose to export it.

Exporting your data

The app lets you export your training history as a CSV file via your device's share sheet. Where that file goes is entirely your choice; BumpSet is not involved once you share it.

Device backup

If you have Android's automatic backup enabled, your on-device app data may be backed up to your own Google account, governed by Google's Privacy Policy. This is a feature of your device and Google account, not something BumpSet collects or controls.

Permissions

BumpSet requests permission to show notifications, display an overlay ("Display over other apps") for the rest timer, and run a foreground service during a workout. These are used solely to show your active workout and rest timer. They involve no data collection.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and collects no data from anyone.

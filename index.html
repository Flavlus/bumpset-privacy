BumpSet Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

BumpSet ("the app") is a workout tracker. This policy explains what data the app handles. In short: everything stays on your device, and we collect nothing.

Data we collect

None. BumpSet has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or advertising SDKs. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal or usage data.

Data the app stores on your device

Your workout templates, logged sets, and preferences are saved in a local database on your device only. This data never leaves your phone unless you choose to export it.

Exporting your data

The app lets you export your training history as a CSV file via your device's share sheet. Where that file goes is entirely your choice; BumpSet is not involved once you share it.

Device backup

If you have Android's automatic backup enabled, your on-device app data may be backed up to your own Google account, governed by Google's Privacy Policy. This is a feature of your device and Google account, not something BumpSet collects or controls.

Permissions

BumpSet requests permission to show notifications, display an overlay ("Display over other apps") for the rest timer, and run a foreground service during a workout. These are used solely to show your active workout and rest timer. They involve no data collection.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and collects no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
